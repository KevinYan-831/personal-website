Every Court Has a Story

An NGO dedicated to revitalizing underused and neglected community sports spaces to promote inclusion, connection, and well-being through sport. Organized 20+ volunteers to renovate 5 basketball courts, hosted 3 community tournaments, and curated a photography exhibition featuring 60+ works celebrating community and resilience. Raised $2k+ through holding charitable basketball tournaments as the renovation and maintenance fund. Built a community of mutual help and support through sports (200+ participants, mostly migrant workers and their children).
